Ecoture
The creation of Ecoture came from the idea of how fast fashion is a large contributor to global warming yet we don't realize what impact it might have on our carbon footprint.
The project began with brainstorming of what the essence of the web app should be about, our planet. Through the use of the tool figma early designs for the wireframe of the app was created. from there creating prototypes of the final app was the main priority.
After the final stage of app prototyping it came time to translate it into code. The biggest feat of this webapp came in the form of it's interactive elements and visuals. There was a heavy use of React in the project to accomplish the look and function of each set during the test.
Once the app had reached a certain level of functionality it came time for user testing to determine if the app flowed nicely. The testing involved five individuals that went through the app and would be questioned at each point of the test about the apps functionality, design, concept, and accessibility. Afterwards the tester would be questioned on their overall experiance and if there was any changes they would recommend. A majority of tester had a postive experiance using that app which proved it was in it's final stages
