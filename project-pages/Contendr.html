Contendr
Contendr came from the idea of a social media app where you could invite anyone to a challenge of your own creation and the public would decide on the winner.
With the focus on creating a strong concept for an app, the ideation process was very thorough. That includes plotting out each role and interaction users would have within Contendr.
For the design of Contendr the goal was to give users a clean and concise interface so they can easily go from scrolling through challenges to posting their own. After multiple prototypes the final look was acheived. The logo and other SVG elements were created in Illustrator to acheive the final version of the app.
For the marketing page of the site a promotional video was required. The result was a short promo made in Adobe After Effects to show off the style and concept of the app.
